DELETE FROM WITHIN THE APP

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data is directly inside the app:

  1. Open Draft Punk and sign in
  2. Tap the ··· menu in the top-right corner
  3. Tap Account
  4. Scroll to the Danger Zone and tap DELETE ACCOUNT

This immediately and permanently removes your account, username, and all cloud data from our servers. Your local data on this device will also be cleared.

REQUEST DELETION BY EMAIL

If you are unable to access the app, you can request deletion by emailing us at:

sulkmediaproduction@gmail.com

Include the email address associated with your Google account. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

WHAT GETS DELETED

  • Your account and username
  • All writing data, notes, characters, and beat notes synced to the cloud
  • Word count history and progress stats
  • Friend connections, messages, and shared content

Local data stored on your device (browser localStorage) is cleared automatically when you delete via the app. If you request deletion by email, you can clear local data manually by clearing your browser/app storage.
